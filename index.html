Theory & Derivation

This surface plots the Hermite–Gaussian eigenfunctions of the isotropic 2D quantum harmonic oscillator: \( \psi_{n_x,n_y}(x,y) = \phi_{n_x}(x)\,\phi_{n_y}(y) \).

Each one-dimensional mode is given by \( \phi_n(\xi) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2^n n!}} H_n(\xi)\, e^{-\xi^2/2} \), and the energy spectrum follows \( E_{n_x,n_y} = (n_x+n_y+1)\hbar\omega \).
